## 📖 Lesson 3 — Comparisons

Comparisons return a `bool` — `True` or `False`.

| Operator | Meaning |
|----------|--------|
| `==` | Equal to |
| `!=` | Not equal to |
| `<` | Less than |
| `>` | Greater than |
| `<=` | Less or equal |
| `>=` | Greater or equal |

> ⚠️ `=` means **store** a value. `==` means **compare**. Do not mix them up!
