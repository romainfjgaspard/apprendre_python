## 📖 Lesson 2 — Math operators

| Operator | What it does | Example | Result |
|----------|-------------|---------|--------|
| `+` | Addition | `10 + 3` | `13` |
| `-` | Subtraction | `10 - 3` | `7` |
| `*` | Multiplication | `10 * 3` | `30` |
| `/` | Division | `10 / 3` | `3.333` |
| `//` | Integer division | `10 // 3` | `3` |
| `%` | Remainder | `10 % 3` | `1` |
| `**` | Power | `2 ** 3` | `8` |

> 💡 `/` always gives a `float`. `//` always gives an `int` (rounded down).
