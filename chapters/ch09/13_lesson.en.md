## 🌟 Bonus Challenges (optional!)

If you want to go further, try these:

1. **Team battle** — Each player has 3 Pokémon. When one faints, the next one comes in.
2. **Choose your moves** — Each Pokémon has 4 moves with different power levels.
3. **Level system** — Pokémon gain XP after winning and level up.
4. **Evolution** — At certain levels, a Pokémon evolves (changes stats and name).
5. **Pokédex viewer** — Use `input()` to let the player search for a Pokémon by name.

These are open-ended — there is no single right answer. Be creative! 🚀
