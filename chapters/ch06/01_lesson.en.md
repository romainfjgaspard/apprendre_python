# 📖 Chapter 6 — Dictionaries

A **dictionary** (`dict`) maps **keys** to **values** — like a real
Pokédex where you look up a Pokémon by name and get its info!

**What you will learn:**
- Create a dict and access values by key
- Add, update, and loop through a dict
- Nested dicts and lists of dicts
- Build a Pokédex!
