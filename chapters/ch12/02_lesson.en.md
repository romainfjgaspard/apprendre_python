## 📖 Lesson 1 — `import` a module

A **module** is a toolbox. To use it, write `import` at the top of your file.
```python
import random
random.randint(1, 6)   # random number from 1 to 6 (like a die)
random.choice([...])   # pick one item from a list
random.shuffle(list)   # mix the list in place
```
